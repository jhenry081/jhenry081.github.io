My Journey In Tech

A real adventure

I don’t remember when I actually started to use a computer, but it surely is a long time. I would mostly open paint, play games (mavis beacon, cactus bruce and the corporate monkeys etc :) ), simpler times they were…Anyways, during my early primary school days, in computer class we would be given a task of opening paint, doing something, and closing it ( You get 100% for doing that) *sigh* But by doing this, I was getting more and more computer literate. Fast-forward 2014 when I was in Secondary School, we learned “Web Development”, we used just HTML. And at that moment I knew, this was the one for me. It was love at first sight (clearly I didn’t know what was in store). I remember when I made a text move using marquee :) I was sooo happy I couldn’t believe I was able to do this, Oh the joy. On completing HTML basics, I wondered was beyond, what more could I do… But that’s a story for another day, Until then…
